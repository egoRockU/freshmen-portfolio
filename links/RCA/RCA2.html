Anchored Hyperlinks

The A stands for Anchor.
In general, the anchor tag tells the browser to anchor or to attach to something else.
Every Anchor tag must have a closing or end tag () to signal the end of the anchor.
HREF stands for Hypertext REFerence. It means that "this is where link is going to".
